Leisure & Cultural Activities

Leisure & Cultural Activities @Tepantar

Tepantar Eco Theatre Resort is a small village in Satkahunia - Purba Bardhaman district. This Eco village is naturally surrounded by the river Ajoy and Garh Jungle. It has a very rich historical background and one can experience a Heritage Botanical walk here. Every tree has a story to share and the Terracotta temples are fascinating with intricate artwork. It is a perfect getaway from Kolkata, Asansol, Purulia, Durgapur and Bardhaman with comfortable Eco stay and AuthenticBengali cuisine. It's pretty near to the land of Rabindranath and the Bauls and also has a lot of options for sightseeing and leisure activities.

Live Theatre

A close connection to Bengali souls Since the Bangaliana Era Tepantar has been successful in keeping this heritage alive., source of entertainment with a social message . Ebong Amra-the famous Theatre Group owns and runs this resort with a team of Theare Artists .The proceeds from Tepantar helps them to generate funds to maintain this cultural heritage. There is an open air stage (40 feet x 30 feet), a Black Box Theatre, rehearsal and workshop space (900 square feet), cottage

Tribal Dance

a) Raibeshe-A folk martial dance performed mostly in birbhum,Murshidabad and Burdwan but Tepantar offers a glimpse into this dance forms that involves vigorous movements along with Acrobatics. This dance is accompanied by Dhols and Kanshis from the Medieval Period.

b) Santhali Dance-Its an immensely vibrant and colourful dance showcasing glories of nature and other social issues.

c) Chou Nrityo

Baul Song

This community has no bindings in faith or religion. Rather they consider music as their religion and the soulful rendition of the Bauls can transport us to serenity.
