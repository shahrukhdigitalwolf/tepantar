Weekend destination - Tepantar Eco Theatre Resort

Very close to the land of Rabindranath and the Bauls is this Theatre Village Resort called Tepantar. This was formed by a theatre group called Ebong Amra . The set up was planned in such a way that one will feel that it is part of the natural ambience of that area.The entire 5 acre premise is laden with a rich botanical heritage with threes more than 100 years old and the flora and fauna invite a lot of birds and different varieties of butterflies which are a feast for the eyes. Comfortable Eco stay, Authentic Bengali cuisine, Baul nights and various leisure activities, historical sight seeing on a pocket friendly budget make Tepantar a must go Weekend getaway .
