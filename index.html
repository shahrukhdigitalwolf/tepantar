Weekend destination - Tepantar Eco Theatre Resort

Very close to the land of Rabindranath and the Bauls is this Theatre Village Resort called Tepantar. This was formed by a theatre group called Ebong Amra . The set up was planned in such a way that one will feel that it is part of the natural ambience of that area.The entire 5 acre premise is laden with a rich botanical heritage with threes more than 100 years old and the flora and fauna invite a lot of birds and different varieties of butterflies which are a feast for the eyes. Comfortable Eco stay, Authentic Bengali cuisine, Baul nights and various leisure activities, historical sight seeing on a pocket friendly budget make Tepantar a must go Weekend getaway .

After your breakfast engage in a trek to Garh Jungle or pay a visit to Shantiniketan and breathe in the culture that Tagore had set in for us.

Tepantar will not let you be bored,read your favourite novel sitting under a shady tree or enjoy your choice of songs during a campfire or try a match of badminton with your bestie.

Tepantar has culture in its foundation itself and is also surrounded by a rich cultural heritage being near to the land of Tagore, Terracotta temples and the Bauls.

Baro maashe Tero Parbon is what Bengalis say-Tepantar replicates it Basanta Utsab,Durga Pujo,Peethe Puli Utsab etc

Gram chaara oi ranga matir poth-and lines up beside that road are neatly done mud huts,and as you move on you can spot an old man just resting below an old Banyan tree with goats grazing around or might spot a lady dressed in Atpoure saree walking barefoot,the beauty lies in the simplicity of these lives.
